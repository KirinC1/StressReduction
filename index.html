Stress Reduction

How I Relieve My Stress Caused by COVID-19

The Main Way is Video Games

Video games are a great way for me to relieve stress. The fact that we might not go back to school for the rest of the school year is daunting.

I have been spending my time playing some Minecraft with some friends on a private server. And today, I started playing Animal Crossing They are both very soothing games and I feel like they definitely relieve some of my stress and take my mind off of COVID-19.

There are many other games that I've playing during this shelter in place, but I feel like these two are the most relaxing ones and I've had the most fun playing them.

What I Look Forward To

I look forward to finally be able to go and hang out with some of my friends again. Also, it will be great when all of the non-essential stores are reopened like stores at the mall. I am also looking forward to going out to eat with my family after all of the restrictions are lowered.
